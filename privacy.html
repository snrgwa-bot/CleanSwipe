Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Summary: CleanSwipe respects your privacy. Your photos never leave your device, and we don't collect any personal data.

Introduction

CleanSwipe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application CleanSwipe (the "App").

Information We Don't Collect

CleanSwipe is designed with privacy at its core. We want to be completely transparent:

  • Photos: Your photos are processed entirely on your device. We never upload, transmit, or access your photos on any server.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers through the App.
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you use the App, which photos you view, or any behavioral data.
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information.

Photo Library Access

CleanSwipe requires access to your photo library to function. This permission is used solely to:

  • Display your photos within the App for review
  • Mark photos as favorites in your device's Photos app
  • Delete photos you choose to remove (with your explicit confirmation)

All photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are never transmitted to external servers.

Data Storage

The App stores minimal data locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms:

  • Your preferences and settings
  • IDs of photos you've reviewed (to show progress)
  • IDs of photos in your trash queue
  • Statistics like total photos deleted

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

CleanSwipe does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties because we don't collect any information to share.

Children's Privacy

CleanSwipe does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The App does not collect personal information from any users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your photos and app data remain entirely under your control on your device.

You can revoke photo library access at any time through your device's Settings app.
