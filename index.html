Why You'll Love CleanSwipe

👆

Intuitive Gestures

Swipe left to delete, right to keep, up for more options. Double-tap to protect favorites from accidental deletion.

🔒

Favorite Protection

Your favorite photos are automatically protected. They can't be deleted until you unlock them with a double-tap.

🗑️

Safe Trash System

Photos go to trash first, not deleted immediately. Review before permanent deletion. Trash persists even if you close the app.

📅

Smart Filters

Filter photos by year and month. Focus on specific time periods to clean up your library systematically.

↩️

Undo Mistakes

Made a wrong swipe? No problem. Instantly undo your last action and bring the photo back.

🌙

Beautiful Dark Mode

Designed for comfortable viewing with a stunning dark interface that's easy on the eyes.

How It Works

1

Open & Allow Access

Grant CleanSwipe access to your photo library to get started.

2

Swipe Through Photos

Swipe left to mark for deletion, right to keep. It's fun and fast!

3

Review & Delete

Check your trash, then delete permanently when ready.
