Quick Tips

Mark a photo for deletion. It goes to trash, not deleted immediately.

👉

👆

👆👆

Double Tap

Protect or unprotect a photo. Protected photos can't be deleted.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete photos permanently?
Tap the trash icon in the top-right corner to view photos marked for deletion. Review them, then tap "Delete All" to permanently remove them. This action cannot be undone.
Can I recover deleted photos?
Photos in the CleanSwipe trash can be restored anytime before you tap "Delete All." Once permanently deleted, photos cannot be recovered through CleanSwipe. However, they may still be available in your device's "Recently Deleted" album in the Photos app for up to 30 days.
What does protecting a photo do?
Double-tapping a photo marks it as a favorite in your device's Photos app and protects it in CleanSwipe. Protected photos cannot be swiped to trash or deleted until you unprotect them by double-tapping again.
How do I filter photos by date?
Tap the filter button at the top of the screen (shows current filter like "All Photos"). You can choose quick filters or select a specific year and month to focus on photos from that time period.
What happens if I close the app?
Your progress is saved automatically. Photos in your trash, your review history, and all settings persist even if you close the app. The trash icon will shake to remind you if you have photos waiting to be deleted.
How do I undo a swipe?
Tap the yellow undo button (↩️) below the photo card to restore your last swiped photo. This works for both left (delete) and right (keep) swipes.
Does CleanSwipe upload my photos anywhere?
No. CleanSwipe works entirely on your device. Your photos never leave your phone and are never uploaded to any server. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
How do I share a photo to another app?
Swipe up on any photo to open options, then tap "Share / Open In..." This opens the iOS share sheet where you can send the photo to any app, save it, AirDrop it, and more.
